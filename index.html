Omar Abd El-Aziz

Finding balance through forceful actions; Dare to go for it an experience that will be remarkable for this generation. An experience filled with dreams and ambitions that will make us the best in everything to be the re-equilibration for AIESEC in Egypt for many upcoming years.

Celebrating an impressive 51-year milestone, AIESEC Egypt has etched a legacy of history, glory, and profound impact. Through the ebb and flow of fifty-one years, this entity has been a crucible of success, a repository of memories, and a nurturing ground for thousands of young leaders. It's a journey marked by the investment of blood, sweat, and tears, shaping AIESEC Egypt through both its triumphs and challenges.

A heartfelt thank you is extended to the passionate change leaders who boldly stepped forward to apply for MC Egypt. Their decision is a testament to their courage and unwavering belief in this movement that commenced 51 years ago—a movement with the potential to transform countless lives, including their own. This new phase, placing people at its core, is poised to redefine the purpose of every endeavor, aspiring to make AIESEC Egypt reach the balance and a spark for positive change in the country.

Now, as the organization looks towards the future, the time is ripe to usher in a new generation of young, purposeful leaders. These individuals are called upon to contribute their all, ensuring that the AIESEC movement thrives for another 51 years and beyond. From the depths of the heart, the wish for the best of luck resonates, acknowledging the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

In commemorating this significant anniversary, may AIESEC Egypt continue to evolve, impact life, and serve as a beacon for positive change. Here's to the enduring success of the organization in the next 51 years and beyond.

Questionnaire

  • I AM A LEADER

    1. Who are you? What do you aspire to become through AIESEC? Why are you applying for MC 24.25 of AIESEC in Egypt?
    2. What are your key strengths and weaknesses? What are your top 3 personal values and unique contributions? How will they influence your term, the AIESEC Egypt network, and the MC Team?
    3. Please mention your previous AIESEC Experiences and Mention 3 achievements, and 3 failures from your AIESEC journey.
    4. What is your leadership style and how will it affect AIESEC in Egypt?
    5. If you would choose a leader, dead or alive to represent you, who would he/she be? Find 5 similarities and explain how and why you see yourselves matching?
    6. If you were given the chance to lead the world for 1 month, what would you do, what would you change from the past and what would you change for the future?
    7. AIESEC often encounters changes and challenges. How do you demonstrate adaptability and resilience as a team member when facing unexpected situations? Describe your role within the next MC team. How do you contribute to positive team dynamics and a collaborative working environment?

  • I AM AN AIESECER

    1. Evaluate the impact of MC teams, 21|22, 22|23, 23|24 national strategies, and their implementation in the network.
    2. What have we learned as a global network from AIESEC 2015 and 2020 mid-term ambitions? How do we take those learnings forward into our 2025 mid-term ambition? If you got the chance to create the 25-30 new ambitions what would they be?
    3. Study the graph of growth and decline trends in the number of exchanges AIESEC Egypt delivered from the year 2015 till 2023, and provide the reasons, external or internal for growth or decline (in one sentence for each year).
    4. Create a 1-year detailed plan with a timeline of how AIESEC in Egypt becomes more relevant and starts to impact Egypt through every single exchange we do including the issues you will work on?
    5. How do you envision, AIESEC in Egypt will be remembered for the 2024|2025 term? Describe the main achievements and milestones for the team.
    6. Create and design a roadmap for AIESEC in Egypt for the term 24|25. Including (Strategies, projects, KPIs, and targets)
    7. AIESEC is a global organization. How has being an AIESECer broadened your global perspective and understanding of international issues? (Include all the crisis that are occurring in the world recently)
    8. What aspects of being an AIESECer bring you the most fulfillment and satisfaction? How do you find purpose in your contributions to the organization? What are new things you want to see happening in the Organization

  • ORGANIZATIONAL LONG TERM STRATEGY AND DEVELOPMENT

    1. Assess AIESEC in Egypt culture and propose the culture you want to implement, including a change management plan. Introduce new CAM
    2. Assess the state of all AIESEC in Egypt expansions, and provide a plan to have all Expansions jump to full membership LCs in one year.
    3. If you will design a new OD Model for AIESEC Egypt, what will be the most relevant metrics that would ensure the growth of AIESEC Egypt's LCs? What will be the KPIs and MoS of each element in the metrics?
    4. How can we create a better R&R system for AIESEC Egypt that can improve AIESEC Egypt membership performance in all products and back office, while maintaining healthy competition between the network?
    5. Create a detailed timeline to make AIESEC Egypt top 1 globally, including (not limited to): National and local conferences, Operational and functional visits, LCPs, and EBs selection process and transition.
    6. Create coaching systems and routines, and a network management plan.
    7. How do you conduct a comprehensive analysis of the external environment to inform AIESEC in Egypt long-term strategy? What factors do you consider in your analysis? How do you establish and measure key performance indicators (KPIs) for AIESEC's long-term strategy? How do you evaluate the success of strategic initiatives?
    8. How will you ensure the involvement of our network in the entity's direction in order to have a quite powerful direction that the entity goes for?

  • HUMAN RESOUCRES MANAGEMENT

    1. A consumer-flow-based consumer Journey review for all AIESEC member durations. What are the main gaps and changes we need to make? Set the action plan for its implementation and describe the synergy's role in making this happen.
    2. How do you see the impact of Membership experience on AIESEC in Egypt? How can you capitalize on the MXP processes (funnel journey)?
    3. If you will design a new performance system, what will be the deliverables of this system and the metrics that would evaluate and assess our members' performance as well as solve the gaps they possess?
    4. Create a Profiling system for AIESEC in Egypt Membership.
    5. Assess the level of education for AIESEC Egypt membership and how can we ensure delivering basic general and functional education for every single member in AIESEC Egypt, and how can utilize local conferences to ensure that. (Utilization plan for prime)
    6. Create a detailed internal communication plan for AIESEC Egypt, that can help in implementing your vision for membership experience, and increase performance, highlighting the campaign's messages timeline and channels.
    7. How do you plan to develop and implement programs that support the continuous development of AIESEC members, ensuring a pipeline of future leaders?

  • ORGANIZATIONAL HEALTH AND RISK MANAGEMENT

    1. What does it mean to you to have all entities sustainable? Based on your answer, how do you assess overall AIESEC's sustainability currently? Why do you think that it is this way?
    2. What are the main risks and potential crises that might affect AIESEC in the near future? How would you avoid/ minimize their impact on the entity?
    3. What are the biggest challenges in ﬁnance and legal in 23|24? How would you overcome them in the year 24|25?
    4. What would be the key indicators you would measure from all entities to know how healthy is AIESEC? How would you collect data? What is the analysis you would do with the data?
    5. Propose 3 new sources of revenue and 3 cost-cutting strategies.
    6. Create a utilization plan for EFB and draw the line between the responsibilities of MC and EFB in financial management.

  • ORGANIZATIONAL PRODUCT STRATEGY

    1. Choose an ELD Product (In case you apply for OGT/IGT, Consider them as two separate products [GTa & GTe]): if you are applying for the Head of exchange role you will need to answer for all 6 ELD Products
      a. Do a funnel analysis for the product. What are the main gaps in the funnel? What do we need to change to fix these gaps? Set an action plan for the implementation of these changes and describe the required synergies for ensuring this happens
      b. Do a supply and demand analysis for the product. What are our main IR partners, and their performance together on each phase in the customer flow? Define the top 5 IRs and the strategies to grow with them. Define the main focus projects *in case you apply for ICX*, and how you will maximize the utilization of the market.
      c. Study the growth of the product and our market share over the past 5 years, and analyze what makes top entities in this year have this market share. Provide at least 3 ideas to take the biggest market share in the next year.
      d. How are you going to ensure the best experience for all your stakeholders (youth and partners), and how to ensure a minimum of 95% standard delivery in your program?
    2. What are the key areas of responsibility of a Product Head? Benchmark with at least three examples from external organizations and identify how those external examples could be implemented in AIESEC.
    3. Do your network management planning for 24.25 Take into consideration routine, capacity building, communication, and touch-points.
    4. Analyze our market share and how can we take a higher percentage from it, state numerical analysis, major competitors, and action steps that will grow this percentage per product.

  • STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIPS AND PORTFOLIO EVOLUTION

    1. Besides the current product portfolio, what additional products would you implement to engage our partners in a more strategic and impactful way? State at least 3 new products.
    2. How can AIESEC in Egypt be branded as a leadership development organization through partnerships? What will your contribution be like? What kind of partnerships will make this happen?
    3. How can the ER department support operational upscaling and help by finding solutions to make more exchanges through partnerships, please provide a minimum of 5 Ideas.
    4. Provide a detailed assessment of the campus state of AIESEC in Egypt. Provide at least three strategies on how to access 100% of Egypt's campuses for PR and BD partners.
    5. Why should we do offline marketing? Mention at least three strategies of offline marketing from the external market and how we integrate them in AIESEC in Egypt.
    6. Do SWOT analysis of the current EwA initiatives, mention if these initiatives help upscale operations. What are the strategies to create initiatives that ensure offline exposure for the organization?
    7. How has AIESEC's portfolio of activities evolved over the years?, Can you highlight key milestones or changes in the organization's focus areas?
    8. How does AIESEC gather feedback from its stakeholders regarding its portfolio of activities?,Have there been instances where stakeholder input led to adjustments in the organization's offerings?

  • ORGANIZATIONAL SYSTEMS AND DIGITAL EXPERIENCE

    1. Which external technology trends should AIESEC capitalize on in the upcoming years? Propose at least two concrete initiatives that would incorporate that trend inside of our technology strategy. Choose one of them and provide a plan for how you will implement it in the entity next year.
    2. Mention all types of Data that we gather and use in AIESEC in Egypt, How to visualize this data to have the best analysis for all operations and resources we have. How does this impact AIESEC in Egypt for the next year? (Don’t answer this question if you are applying for Brand)
    3. If you choose one thing (whatever it is, a platform or a project) to work on throughout the term, what is it? And how this will impact AIESEC in Egypt in its next term vision, to help it be in its next stage.
    4. Mention all types of users that use our platforms. Provide a detailed analysis for all the platforms at least but not limited to their user experience and their features. Mention how it can be improved.
    5. Provide a roadmap for the Digital Experience and Information Management of AIESEC in Egypt for the next three years.
    6. Build a pipeline plan that will ensure the sustainability of the entity in the next term. (Build the plan for IM if you are applying for this position, for Brand if you are applying for this position)
    7. Assess the digital marketing we do in AIESEC in Egypt, mentioning at least three strategies for brand positioning online for all of our current social media platforms and potential online social media platforms.

  • MARKETS LAUNCHING AND EXPANDING

    1. Create a full market research for Egypt’s campuses Mention potential 5 markets from the universities of Egypt, that you see as potential to expand in, and provide a detailed plan on how these markets can turn into a startup in 6 months, including and not limited to: timeline, HR, and physical and digital access, and goals for each market expansion.
    2. What will be your criteria for selecting the Market expansion manager, and your plan to ensure getting the needed HR capacity from the campuses?
    3. Mention 5 potential entities from the international network to grow with, create a utilization plan for each of them, and calculate the ROI of the financial investments for the ceederships.
    4. Study the relevance between the role of “growth” and the external market and the importance of it to organizational growth, and mention 3 external references from organizations that benefit from market expansions.
    5. How can you ensure getting a minimum 80% ICX market share, from all MEA region operations through expanding in New Markets?
    6. Create an assessment for the potential entities that can have mutual planning and you can grow with (Analyze everything that can support in planning to expand in those countries)
